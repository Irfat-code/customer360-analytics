# Business Overview

## Problem
Customer Success teams typically work across multiple disconnected tools: a CRM for account data, a billing system for subscriptions, a helpdesk for support tickets, product analytics for usage, and a separate survey tool for NPS. Without a unified view, prioritizing which accounts need attention today is largely manual and inconsistent across the team.

## Target users
- Customer Success Managers, who need a prioritized list of accounts to act on
- CS leadership, who need visibility into portfolio-wide health and risk trends
- Sales and Account teams, who need to identify expansion-ready accounts

## Key metrics this platform produces
- **Customer health score** (0-100): a composite signal of subscription status, support burden, and satisfaction
- **Churn risk category** (Low/Medium/High/Critical): a forward-looking risk classification
- **Recommended action, owner, and SLA**: a concrete, assignable next step per account

## Success criteria
A Customer Success Manager should be able to open the `recommended_actions` table, filter to Critical and High risk, and immediately know who to contact, why, and how urgently — without needing to cross-reference four other systems first.
